Doggy Sweater

This is a sweater I made for Sky to wear when she's cheering on the Toronto Maple Leafs. I designed the pattern myself, and used fabric from jersey deadstock that I found. I sewed a Maple Leafs logo to the back using offcuts from a t-shirt I cropped. Sky loves wearing it, especially on game days!
