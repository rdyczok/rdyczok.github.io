Rope Bag

This rope bag has a built in tarp so I can easily flake my rope and be ready to climb! I reinforced the corners of the tarp with an extra layer of fabric, and colour coded them with bias tape so they are distinguisable. The base fabric is a study water resistant synthetic fabric to keep the rope safe, and I added handles and a drawstring so it can easily be packed up and carried away.
